Unified Police Department

605 Officer Amy Cortez reporting on 914 Officer Chad Gable

First impressions of Officer Chad Gable are…troubling. In an interview with Sheriff Torretti, one of the first things he admitted about himself was his extensive brain damage. This has quite an impact on his short term memory (to the point where he does not remember talking back to the Sheriff the day prior), and the jury’s still out on how it impacts his long term memory. He claims to still be able to know right from wrong, yet also claims that he killed his mother.

Here are some observations I’ve made after spending the day with Officer Gable:

  • Was the only cop on scene who insisted on not helping the criminals rob the (daily pre-storm) vault.
  • He’s quite decent at paralleling and can lead others to intercept suspects at the correct spots.
  • Angel said she was “proud of him” over the radio.
  • His brain damage sometimes causes him to spontaneously change accents.
  • Successfully spiked a vehicle, and not other cops (wow).
  • Threatened a suspect that he would “come in there” (the jail cell).
  • RTO’d Alex Casterman which made him sign off duty.
  • Baited Connell MacGregor into shooting us (he claims he “out-petty’d” MacGregor which resulted in us getting shot).
  • Updated his own profile (very biased).
  • He would like to make it known that he is dating Penny Farthing (not single) and that he is honest and very loyal.
  • Overall impressive scene-leading ability.

Overall, I believe that Officer Gable is a decently competent enforcer of the law and would be a great addition to the SDSO/BCSO. His choice of words may get him in trouble often, but he ultimately makes the correct decisions when it comes to his police work.
